Star Trails

I went stargazing on 12/22/2020 and took my first star trail photo. Below are the camera settings and steps I recorded for myself.

  • Manual focus, wide aperture f/2.8
  • 24mm wide angle lens
  • ISO 2500 as a starting point
  • 500 rule for exposure time
