I am a Research Scientist at Meta, working on immersive 3D and 4D experiences. My research focuses on high-fidelity view synthesis, combining diffusion models with 3D/4D scene representations.

I received my Ph.D. from Virginia Tech in 2022, where I was advised by Jia-Bin Huang. Before that, I earned my Master’s degree from the University of Michigan and my Bachelor’s degree from Oregon State University.
